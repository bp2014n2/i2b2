Demographics (2 Patient Sets) - Simple Counts

Introduction

This plugin displays demographic information for two patient sets across several different categories.

Instructions

Navigate to the "Specify Data" tab. Then, drag and drop two Patient Sets onto the input boxes. Finally, click the "View Results" tab to view the demographic details.
